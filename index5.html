Why We're Here


We support the ecosystem of people who provide services in the personal injury industry. We believe by
powering them, we help the plaintiffs whom they serve to get a better deal from the justice system.

What we do


Mighty empowers anyone with financial interests in personal
injury cases (aka lienholders) to manage them better. Our law
firm portal makes it easy for lienholders to communicate and
share documents with the law firms that distribute funds when
cases settle.


Who we are


We are engineers, data scientists, underwriters, and sales managers. We care about
the long game of improving the civil justice system, and we get a thrill from solving
problems to make people's lives easier. We have activist hearts and engineering
brains. If that sounds like you, come join us.
